author_bio: 'The [Electronic Schoolhouse / La Escuela Electrónica](http://www.archives.nysed.gov/projects/escuela/index.shtml)
  is a bilingual educational resource of historical resources developed by the [New
  York State Archives](http://www.archives.nysed.gov/aindex.shtml), a program of the
  New York Department of Education that cares for and provides access to more than
  200 million documents.

  With background information available in both English and Spanish, materials examine
  the Latino experience in New York through documents and images from 1861 to the
  present.

  Also available are focus questions, activities, lists of additional resources, and
  instructional videos that explain how teachers can use materials.'
