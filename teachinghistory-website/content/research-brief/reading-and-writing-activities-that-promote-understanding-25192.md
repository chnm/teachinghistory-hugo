* Craft essay assignments (or shorter constructed response activities) that ask students to argue a particular position or make a claim. Questions like: What were the primary causes of the American Civil War? How effective was the New Deal? What is historically significant about the ideology of manifest destiny? require that students do more than simply recount “what happened.”
* Present students with multiple relevant excerpts to answer the question. If possible, include sources that represent multiple perspectives on the historical period or event.
* If you are using a textbook, try using separate excerpts of it in a single task or complementing it with additional sources.
* Ask students to cite several different excerpts or texts in their essays, encouraging them to do the thinking necessary to connect different texts to their claims (and to other texts).
