Federal Resources for Educational Excellence (FREE), a service of the [Department of Education](http://www.ed.gov), categorizes more than 1,600 learning resources from the federal government by subject including history, language arts, math, and science.

Under the umbrella of [History and Social Studies](http://www.free.ed.gov/subjects.cfm?subject_id=178), [U.S. History Topics](http://free.ed.gov/subjects.cfm?subject_id=178&res_feature_request=0) are further subdivided [thematically](http://free.ed.gov/subjects.cfm?subject_id=178&res_feature_request=0) and [chronologically](http://free.ed.gov/subjects.cfm?subject_id=157&res_feature_request=0).
