The Federal Aviation Administration regulates civil aviation to promote safety, encourages and develops civil aeronautics, and develops and operates a system of air traffic control for both civil and military aircraft. The [History of Federal Aviation Administration](http://www.faa.gov/about/history/) section includes a brief history of the FAA, a bibliography, and the online publication *The Federal Aviation Administration: A Historical Perspective, 1903–2008*. The FAA 50th Anniversary page includes an interactive multimedia timeline, which features significant events from the [FAA Historical Chronology.](http://www.faa.gov/about/history/chronolog_history) FAA administrators past and present, aeromedical research contributions, and flight inspection station history are also included.
