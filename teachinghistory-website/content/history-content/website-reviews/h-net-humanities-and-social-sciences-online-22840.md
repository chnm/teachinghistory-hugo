An indispensable resource for teachers and scholars in a wide variety of fields, but especially for historians. H-Net—an international interdisciplinary organization of scholars and teachers—contains sections on [H-Net Reviews](https://networks.h-net.org/browse/reviews), which publishes and disseminates reviews of books, films, museums, software, sound recordings, and websites; [Discussion Networks](http://web.archive.org/web/20221127100124/https://networks.h-net.org/browse/discussions), a gateway to more than 130 academic discussion networks administered by H-Net via email; "H-Net Papers on Teaching and Technology," presenting 10 discussion panels on multimedia teaching; academic announcements of professional organizations, conference programs, fellowships, and prizes; employment listings; and additional websites from various H-Net special projects.
