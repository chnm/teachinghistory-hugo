|  | Dr. Seuss Went to War: A Catalogue of Political Cartoons Did you know that Dr. Seuss (Theodor Seuss Geisel), the author of the childhood classic *Green Eggs and Ham*, also created political cartoons? Explore this UC San Diego collection of more than 400 Seuss cartoons, drawn for the magazine *PM* between 1941 and 1943. The cartoons address a range of issues related to World War II.  [Learn more.](http://teachinghistory.org/history-content/website-reviews/23075) |  |  |  | Teaching with Historical Film Clips Interested in using documentaries or footage of historic events in your classroom? Divided into [elementary](http://teachinghistory.org/teaching-materials/teaching-guides/24312), [middle](http://teachinghistory.org/teaching-materials/teaching-guides/24313), and [high school](http://teachinghistory.org/teaching-materials/teaching-guides/24314) examples, this guide helps teach students to analyze historical footage and documentaries. The guide also features a [list](http://teachinghistory.org/system/files/Archives_of_Historic_Footage.pdf) of open-access film archives with historical footage. [Learn more.](http://teachinghistory.org/teaching-materials/teaching-guides/24299) |  |
