For your convenience, several versions of the teachinghistory.org logo are provided for download below, which can be used for web or print applications. Web versions are in png format. Print versions are in eps format.

In addition, we have created two “badges” for your use on your website: The first one indicates that your site has been reviewed on teachinghistory.org. If used, we would appreciate your including a link from your site to the review here at http://teachinghistory.org. The second is a generic badge to use on a web page.

[Download the teachinghistory.org badge »   ![](/files/th-logo-badge.png)](/files/th-logo-badge.png)

[Download a zip archive of the teachinghistory badge and logos »](/sites/default/files/TeachingHistory-logos-badge.zip)

#### Guidelines

Please do not distort or recolor the logo, or apply textures or other filters to it. Please allow at least one-quarter inch, or 18 pixels, of clear space around the logo.
