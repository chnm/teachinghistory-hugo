Favorites lets you collect items on Teachinghistory.org.
***How?*** To save an item, click the *Add Favorite* icon. The selected item will appear in your Favorites box.
***Change your mind?*** Click the orange **X** and the link will disappear.
***Adding ideas?*** Want to write yourself a note? To remember why you selected a particular page? Click on the pencil next to your selection and write your note in the annotation box.
***Finished?*** Your favorites will be waiting for you when you return using the same computer and browser. If you want to take your favorites with you, click the envelope icon, enter an email address, and click *Email Favorites*. You've now forwarded all of your selections and notes to your email account.
**Please note**: If you clear your browser history, your favorites will also be cleared on Teachinghistory.org. They will still be accessible if you sent yourself an email.
