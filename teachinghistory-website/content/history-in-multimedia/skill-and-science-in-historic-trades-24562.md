Harmony Hunter meets with Jay Gaynor the Director of Colonial Williamsburg's Historic Trades to discuss how Colonial Williamsburg attempts to preserve and reproduce 18th-century economic situations. Specifically, Gaynor explains how colonial-style craftsmanship and trade is present in Colonial Williamsburg.
