In this interview from July 31, 1986, Tim Maby reports on the findings of Dr Ballard's team and describes the amazing footage they captured of the wreck of the Titanic.
This BBC interview series also features a [related interview](http://teachinghistory.org/history-content/history-in-multimedia/25558) in which reporter Dr. Ballard and Mark Newhart describe the underwater camera used to take these impressive photographs.
