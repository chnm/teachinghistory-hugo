Colonial Williamburg's Harmony Hunter interviews author and historian Pauline Maier to discover more about the ratification and malleability of the United States Constitution.
The podcast also has an accompanying video, or vodcast, on the Constitution that can be viewed [here](http://www.history.org/media/videoplayer/?cat=vodcast&file=ConstitutionDay).
The Colonial Williamsburg site also offers more resources on the Constitution, such as [transcript](http://www.history.org/Almanack/life/politics/constitu.cfm) of the text and a link to a site where you can explore the [original document](http://research.history.org/pf/documents/constitution.cfm).
