In this lecture originally given at the New-York Historical Society, Pulitzer Prize-winning historian Laurel Thatcher Ulrich discusses her book *The Age of Homespun: Objects and Stories in the Creation of an American Myth*. Thatcher provides an overview of her research process and discusses the material artifacts she selected to include in her book.
