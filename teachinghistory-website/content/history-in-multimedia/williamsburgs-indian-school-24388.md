Colonial Williamsburg's Harmony Hunter interviews William and Mary professor Jim Axtell to discover more about the Indian School, called [Brafferton](http://www.history.org/almanack/places/hb/hbbraff.cfm), that was build in 1723 on the campus of the [College of William and Mary](http://www.wm.edu/about/history/historiccampus/indianschool/index.php). Axtell discusses the school's primary function as a means to propagate the Christian faith among the Native Americans near Williamsburg.
