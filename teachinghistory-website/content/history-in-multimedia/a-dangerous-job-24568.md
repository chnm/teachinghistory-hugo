In this podcast, Harmony Hunter interviews James Ingram, member of Colonial Williamsburg's Nation Builders program. The Nation Builders program is about giving a voice to more than just well-known historical figures in America's history. Specifically, Ingram discusses his portrayal of Gowan Pamphlet, the first ordained slave preacher in America.
Interested listeners can learn more about Gowan Pamphlet by clicking [here](http://www.history.org/almanack/people/bios/biopam.cfm) for biographical information on the slave preacher.
Or would you rather learn more about colonial religion in general? Colonial Williamsburg also offers an [overview](http://www.history.org/Almanack/life/religion/religionhdr.cfm) of various aspects of 18th century religious beliefs and practices.
