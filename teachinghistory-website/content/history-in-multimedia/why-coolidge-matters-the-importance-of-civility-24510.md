Authors James Douglas, Milton G. Valera, and Patrick J. Leahy make a compelling case for revisiting and learning from the moral tenor and style that defined the Coolidge presidency. The authors also discuss their new book, *Why Coolidge Matters: How Civility in Politics Can Bring a Nation Together*.
