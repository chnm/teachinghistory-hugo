Architectural historian Carl Lounsbury describes the [Bodleian Plate](http://www.history.org/history/teaching/enewsletter/volume4/july06/primsource.cfm), a copper plate preserving an illustration of Williamsburg in 1747. The Plate has been used as reference in the restoration of Colonial Williamsburg.
