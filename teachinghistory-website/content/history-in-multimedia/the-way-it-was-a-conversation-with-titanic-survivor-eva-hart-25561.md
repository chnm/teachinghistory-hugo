In this interview from the BBC series entitled "Survivors of the Titanic," Eva Hart shares her personal account of the disaster in vivid detail—from her mother's first premonitions of doom to the years after the tragedy, which saw her become a minor celebrity. The interview was originally conducted on April 11, 1987.
