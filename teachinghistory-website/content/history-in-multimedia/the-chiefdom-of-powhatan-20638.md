Buck Woodard of Colonial Williamsburg describes the population and social organization of Native American society in the Chesapeake area prior to and during the arrival of the Jamestown colonists, focusing particularly on Powhatan and the [Powhatan Paramount Chiefdom](http://www.history.org/history/teaching/enewsletter/volume2/november03/cultures.cfm).
