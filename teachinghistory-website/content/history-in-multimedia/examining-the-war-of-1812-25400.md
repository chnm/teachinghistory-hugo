In this video series, HISTORY.com takes viewers on a journey to better understand the War of 1812. Topics discussed include Dolly Madison's rescue of a portrait of George Washington, Francis Scott Key's composition of "The Star-Spangled Banner," and James Madison's prominence in the onset of hostilities. Be sure to check out all [eight videos](http://www.history.com/topics/war-of-1812/videos#james-madison-and-the-war-of-1812) to truly experience this important event in American history.
