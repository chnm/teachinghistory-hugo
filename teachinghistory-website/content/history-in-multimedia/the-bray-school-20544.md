Headmistress [Ann Wager](http://research.history.org/Historical_Research/Research_Themes/ThemeReligion/Wager.cfm) taught at the Bray School in Williamsburg, VA, from 1760 to 1774, educating enslaved children. Interpreter Antoinette Brennan shares details from Wager's life and describes the school and its operations.
