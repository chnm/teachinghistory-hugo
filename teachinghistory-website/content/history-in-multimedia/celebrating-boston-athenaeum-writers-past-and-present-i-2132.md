As part of a bicentennial series celebrating Boston Athenaeum writers, Jill Ker Conway discusses the work of maritime historian Samuel Eliot Morison (1887–1976); Megan Marshall discusses Christian author and first female entrant to the Athenaeum, Hannah Adams (1755–1831); and Philip McFarland discusses historian Francis Parkman (1823–1893).
