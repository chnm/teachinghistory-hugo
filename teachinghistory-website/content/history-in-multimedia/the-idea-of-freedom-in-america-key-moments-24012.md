From the Gilder Lehrman Institute:

Orlando Patterson, John Cowles Professor of Sociology at Harvard University, discusses the way in which the concept of freedom has shifted throughout American history. He talks about traditional public freedoms, like civil liberties, and how they are seen as separate from a highly privatized conception and experience of freedom in everyday life.
