From the Gilder Lehrman Institute:

Larry Kramer, Dean at Stanford Law School, walks his class through the complicated legal twists and turns created by the Court's *Brown v. Board of Education* decision. He explains the ramifications of *Brown* on the various desegregation cases that followed it, up through the 1970s.
