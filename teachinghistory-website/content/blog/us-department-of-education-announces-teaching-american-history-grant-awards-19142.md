The [U.S. Department of Education](http://www.ed.gov) has announced 121 [Teaching American History (TAH) grant awards](http://www2.ed.gov/news/pressreleases/2008/04/04022008.html) to improve the quality of American history education.

School districts in 40 states are the recipients of 121 new grants to improve the quality of American History education, mandated under the No Child Left Behind Act.

Visit the [Teaching American History program site](http://www2.ed.gov/programs/teachinghistory/index.html) for further information.
