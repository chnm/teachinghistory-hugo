The May issue of [*Perspectives*](http://web.archive.org/web/20220128105211/https://www.historians.org/Perspectives/issues/2008/0805/index.cfm) from the [American Historical Association](http://www.historians.org) examines technology in the classroom. [Teaching History with YouTube (and other primary-source video sites on the internet)](http://web.archive.org/web/20130313052916/http://www.historians.org/perspectives/issues/2008/0805/0805tec2.cfm) by Jonathon Rees and [Podcasting and the Profession](http://web.archive.org/web/20130313052916/http://www.historians.org/perspectives/issues/2008/0805/0805tec2.cfm) by Kreita Sigler explore using and creating audiovisuals.
