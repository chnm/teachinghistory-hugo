The Clinton County Historical Society seeks to preserve and share the history of Clinton County, IN. To this end, the society operates a museum of local history. Exhibit topics include military history and historic farming tools.

The society offers exhibits, school tours, and group tours. Reservations are required for school, group, and weekend tours.
