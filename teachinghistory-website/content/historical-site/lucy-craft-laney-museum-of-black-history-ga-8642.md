The Lucy Craft Laney Museum of Black History presents African American history. The gardens contain plants which would have grown during the lifetime of Lucy Craft Laney (1854-1933), the museum's inspiration, a woman devoted to educating African American youths.

The museum offers exhibits, a summer camp, and a period garden.
