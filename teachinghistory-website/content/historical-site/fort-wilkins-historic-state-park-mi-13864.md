Fort Wilkins Historic State Park features a restored 1844 army military outpost and one of the first lighthouses on Lake Superior, built in 1866. Costumed interpreters demonstrate the history of the Keweenaw Peninsula fort.
The park offers demonstrations, tours, exhibits, and recreational and educational events.
