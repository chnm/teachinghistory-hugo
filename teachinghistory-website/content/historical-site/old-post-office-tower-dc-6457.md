The Old Post Office, built between 1892 and 1899, is home to the Bells of Congress, which were a present from England on the nation's Bicentennial. From the observation deck, located 270 feet above the street level, the visitor can get a spectacular view of the nation's capital.

The tower offers exhibits and tours.
