The Linn County Historical Museum is housed in Brownsville's original railroad depot and holds collections and information representing all of Linn County. The museum also cares for the Moyer House, an elegant home completed in 1881 by pioneering figures of Brownsville.
The museum and house offer exhibits, tours, and occasional living history events.
