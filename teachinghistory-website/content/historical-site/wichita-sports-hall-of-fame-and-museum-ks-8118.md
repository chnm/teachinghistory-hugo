The Wichita Sports Hall of Fame and Museum commemorates figures who excelled in sports. To be recognized, these individuals and teams either had to be from Witchita or to have excelled at their sport in Witchita. Collections include uniforms, trophies, sporting equipment, and commemorative plaques.
The museum offers exhibits.
