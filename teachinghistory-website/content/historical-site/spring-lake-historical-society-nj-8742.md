The Spring Lake Historical Society's primary function is to preserve materials which illustrate the history of the Borough of Spring Lake, New Jersey and the surrounding areas. To this end, the society operates a museum of local history, housed in the town's first public school, built 1897.
The society offers museum exhibits.
