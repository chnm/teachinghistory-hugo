The Sullivan County Historical Society seeks to preserve and share the history of Sullivan County, New York, site of the Revolutionary War's Battle of Minisink in 1779. To this end the society operates a local history museum, located within the 1912 Hurleyville School.

The museum offers exhibits, period rooms, lectures, and archival access.
