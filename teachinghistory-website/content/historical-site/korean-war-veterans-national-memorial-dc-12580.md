The Korean War Veterans National Memorial consists of 19 stainless steel sculptures and a granite wall. The site commemorates and honors veterans and the deceased of the Korean War, while simultaneously reminding the viewer of the cost required to maintain freedom.

The site offers only the physical memorial.
