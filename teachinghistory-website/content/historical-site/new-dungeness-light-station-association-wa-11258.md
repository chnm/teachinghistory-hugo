The New Dungeness Light Station Association preserves, restores, and interprets the New Dungeness Light Station, the lighthouse of which has been in use since 1857.
The site offers exhibits on the significance of the lighthouse, a sound broadcast of the turning of the light, and guided tours of the lighthouse.
