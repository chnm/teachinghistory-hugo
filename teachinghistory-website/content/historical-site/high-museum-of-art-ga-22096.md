The High Museum of Art houses a wide range of art collections, including collections of American art from the 19th and 20th centuries, folk art, decorative arts, photography, and modern and contemporary art.
The museum offers exhibits, self-guided and guided tours for school groups, in-school speakers, lectures and other recreational and events, and professional development institutes for educators.
