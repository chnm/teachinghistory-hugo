The Dr. Lester L. Williams Fire Museum largely consists of a private collection of fire memorabilia. Highlights include an 1896 hose wagon; 1898 steam pumper; 1884 hose cart; and historic breathing apparatus, clocks, and fire helmets from 1800 to present.

The museum offers exhibits.
