The Northbrook Historical Society maintains a local history museum in its headquarters, the 1894 Northfield Inn. The lower level contains permanent exhibits, the main floor changing exhibits, and the top floor a recreation of an 1890s home.

The museum offers exhibits, a video tour, programs for school groups (grades 2-4), and research library access.
