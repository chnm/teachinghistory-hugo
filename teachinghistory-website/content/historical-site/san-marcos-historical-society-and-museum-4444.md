The San Marcos Historical Society is a local organization dedicated to the archiving and preservation of the history of San Marcos. It operates a museum, as well as a Heritage Park containing two restored historical structures: the 1888 Cox House and the 1890 Bidwell House.
Both the museum and the park offer tours and exhibits.
