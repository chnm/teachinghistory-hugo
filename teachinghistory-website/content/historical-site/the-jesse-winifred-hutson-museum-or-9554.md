The Jesse & Winifred Hutson Museum is located on the Mt. Hood scenic byway, and offers visitors an impressive collection of rocks, minerals, and Native American artifacts. The museum focuses on the history of the local American Indian tribes and local geology.

The museum offers exhibits, lectures, and presentations. The website offers basic visitor information.
