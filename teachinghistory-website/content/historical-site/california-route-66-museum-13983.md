The California Route 66 Museum presents the history of Route 66 and the communities found along this historic highway. Exhibit topics include construction of Route 66; automobile history; highway culture; the economic impact of this road; and historic Victorville, CA. Highlights include roadside attractions from Hula Ville. Contemporary road culture artwork is also displayed.

The museum offers exhibits, an art gallery, and research library access.
