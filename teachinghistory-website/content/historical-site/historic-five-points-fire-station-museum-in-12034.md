The Historic Five Points Fire Station Museum is located in the 83-year-old Five Points Fire Station. The building has been restored to its 1937 glory, and houses an impressive collection of firefighting memorabilia and equipment.

The museum offers exhibits and guided tours. The website offers fire safety information and visitor information.
