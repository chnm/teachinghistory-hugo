The Anniston Museum of Natural History maintains seven exhibit halls exploring the natural history of Alabama and the globe. Changing exhibits may be relevant to U.S. history topics.
The museum offers exhibits, self-guided and guided tours for school groups, educational resource trunks for loan, and in-class outreach presentations.
