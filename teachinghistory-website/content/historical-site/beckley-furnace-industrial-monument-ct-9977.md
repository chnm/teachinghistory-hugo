The Beckley Iron Furnace once produced iron for the manufacture of railroad car wheels. It is Connecticut's best preserved example of a technology that has long since vanished.

A second website for the site, maintained by the Friends of Beckley, can be found [here.](http://www.beckleyfurnace.org/)

The site offers tours.
