The Apex Historical Society seeks to preserve and share the history of Apex, NC. To this end, the society offers information on historical rural life. This information is located within the Maynard-Pearson House, which dates to the 1870s.

The society offers exhibits, walking tours for students, educational sessions, and tours of the Maynard-Pearson House.
