The Lemhi County Historical Museum preserves the life and times of Sacajawea, Lemhi County's most famous resident, and the more general history of Lemhi County.
The museum offers historical artifacts and exhibits. The website offers visitor information and detailed historical information regarding the life and times of Sacajawea.
