The Etowah Indian Mounds Historic Site is a 54-acre Mississippian Culture site. Inhabited between 1000 and 1550, the acreage contains a plaza; a village; a ditch; and six mounds, used for burial.
The site offers exhibits, an interpretive nature trail, and a picnic area.
