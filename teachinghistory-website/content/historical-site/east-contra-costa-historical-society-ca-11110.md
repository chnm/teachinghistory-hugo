The East Contra Costa Historical Society is located in Brentwood, California, and is headquarted in a historic home that serves as both a local history museum and as a historic house museum.
The museum offers exhibits, period rooms, and guided tours. The website offers photographs of the home, visitor information, and a brief history of the area.
