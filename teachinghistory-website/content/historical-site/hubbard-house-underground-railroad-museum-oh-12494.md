The Hubbard House Underground Railroad Museum presents information on the Underground Railroad, Civil War, and Americana. The museum is housed within the circa 1841 Hubbard House. The first floor depicts 1840s life in the Connecticut Western Reserve.

The museum offers exhibits, period rooms, self-guided tours, and guided tours.
