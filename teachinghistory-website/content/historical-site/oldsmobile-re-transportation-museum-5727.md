From rare vehicles like REO, Star, Durant, and Viking to classic Oldsmobiles, buggies, bicycles, trucks, and engines, the Museum covers the range of transportation history. Lansing, MI, is a city with over a century of building cars and automobile innovation.

The museum offers exhibits and tours.
