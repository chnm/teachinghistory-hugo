The Delaware Sports Museum and Hall of Fame is dedicated to preserving the history and accomplishments of athletes throughout the state of Delaware. The hall of fame was completed in 2002, and is located along the Wilmington waterfront.

The museum offers exhibits, guided tours, and special events. The website offers biographies of all inductees and visitor information.
