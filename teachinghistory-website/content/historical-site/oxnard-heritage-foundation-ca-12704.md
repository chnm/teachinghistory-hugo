The Oxnard Heritage Foundation seeks to preserve and share the history of Oxnard County, California.
The foundation offers guided and self-guided tours of Heritage Square. Group tours are available by prior arrangement. Heritage Square consists of 15 restored buildings dating from 1876 to 1912.
