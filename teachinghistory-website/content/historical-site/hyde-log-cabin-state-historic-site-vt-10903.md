Jedediah Hyde Jr. built the Hyde Log Cabin in 1783. Vermont's oldest log cabin, the Hyde Log Cabin today stands as a historic house museum, and gives visitors a glimpse into the lives of early Vermont settlers.

The cabin offers guided tours. The website offers a biography of Jedediah Hyde Jr., a history of the home, and visitor information.
