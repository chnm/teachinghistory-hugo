The Carlsbad Historical Society operates the Magee House Museum, which is furnished to reflect circa 1900 pioneer life. In addition, the house contains the society archives and local history exhibits.

The society offers exhibits, period rooms, guided garden tours, tour and tea programs, and a third grade educational program.
