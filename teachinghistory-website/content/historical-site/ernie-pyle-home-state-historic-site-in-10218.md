Visit the home of respected WWII reporter Ernie Pyle at his childhood home and farm. The site has a new visitor's center and a host of memorabilia from the journalist's life.

School tours are available by appointment and school programs that meet Indiana State standards for grades K-high school. Teacher packets are also avaliable.
