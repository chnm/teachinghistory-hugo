The East End Seaport Museum and Marine Foundation presents the maritime history of the East End of Long Island, New York. Museum highlights include historic Fresnel lenses. The foundation also operates a reconstructed blacksmith's shop and the 1906 schooner *Mary E*.
The society offers exhibits, lectures, children's summer programs, and blacksmithing demonstrations.
