The Jackson County Historical Society seeks to preserve and share the history of the upper Midwest—with emphasis on Jackson County, Iowa. To this end, the society operates a museum of local history. The museum boasts a strong collection of historical farm equipment.

The society offers exhibits and a weekly brown bag lunch.
