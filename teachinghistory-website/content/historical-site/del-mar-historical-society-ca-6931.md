The Del Mar Historical Society works to collect, preserve, and chronicle the history of Del Mar, California. Del Mar's history is a tapestry of railroads, racehorses, fashionable hotels, sparkling beaches and glamorous Hollywood stars. The society puts out four newsletters a year and has an extensive collection of historical artifacts from Del Mar's history.
The site offers information about the society, an events calendar, a brief historical section about Del Mar, and an online store.
