The Society maintains two facilities. The 1833 Jail Museum, located on the Courthouse Green, houses exhibits featuring artifacts representing various periods of county history. The Historical Center houses the Society's offices, research library, map and photograph collections, and museum shop.

The society offers research library access; the museum offers exhibits.
