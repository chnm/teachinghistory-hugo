The Lincoln Memorial is located on the National Mall in downtown Washington, DC, and commemorates one of our country's finest presidents, Abraham Lincoln. The Memorial is open to visitors year round, and offers occasional special events. Visitors are welcome to take a self-guided tour.

The website offers historical information and visitor information.
