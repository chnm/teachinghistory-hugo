The Darwin Martin House State Historic Site preserves a 1905 house complex designed by Frank Lloyd Wright for the Larkin Soap Company executive Darwin Martin. The complex includes the main house, conservatory, garage-stable, and separate secondary house.

The house offers docent-led tours, year-round, by reservation only.
