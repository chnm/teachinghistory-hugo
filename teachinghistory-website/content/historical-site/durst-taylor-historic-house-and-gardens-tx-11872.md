The Durst-Taylor Historic House and Gardens is an 1830's wood-frame house located in Nacagdoches, Texas. The home is the second oldest structure still standing in Nacagdoches. The house features a visitor center, blacksmith shop, smokehouse, and historic gardens.

The home offers guided tours. The website offers a brief history of the home as well as visitor information.
