The Coffey County Historical Museum presents the history of Coffey County, KS. Collections include antique dolls and a restored 1896 one-room schoolhouse, 1895 church, and Conestoga wagon.

The museum offers exhibits, guided tours, and a research library. Reservations are required for all guided tours. Visitors who do not wish to have a guide do not require reservations.
