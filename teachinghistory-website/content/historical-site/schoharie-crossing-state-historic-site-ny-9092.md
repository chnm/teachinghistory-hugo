The Schoharie Crossing State Historic Site presents the history of the Erie Canal (completed 1825) and the commercial and technological impact which it had on both the state of New York and the nation in its entirety. Structures on site include Putman's Lock Stand and the Scoharie Aqueduct.

The site offers exhibits, educational programs, and outreach programs.
