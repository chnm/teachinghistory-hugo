The Kripplebush Schoolhouse was opened in 1957, and today gives visitors a glimpse into rural life in the mid 19th century. Immediately adjacent to the schoolhouse is the Junior order of Mechanics Lodge Hall.

The museum offers guided tours. The website offers basic visitor information and a brief history of the schoolhouse.
