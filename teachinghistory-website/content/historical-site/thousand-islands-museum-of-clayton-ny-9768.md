The Thousand Islands Museum of Clayton presents the history of Clayton and St. Lawrence River areas, New York. Topics include commerce, hunting, agriculture, and fishing. Collection highlights include historic hunting decoys.

The museum offers a resource room. Arrangements for research assistance must be made in advance, and payment is required.
