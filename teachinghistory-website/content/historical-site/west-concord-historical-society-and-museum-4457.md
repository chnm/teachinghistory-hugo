The West Concord Historical Society and Community Center is located in a former schoolhouse. The museum, with 10 display rooms of history memorabilia, is in the original schoolhouse built in 1902. The Community Center is located in an addition that was built in 1936 and includes an auditorium and additional classroom space.
The museum offers exhibits and tours.
