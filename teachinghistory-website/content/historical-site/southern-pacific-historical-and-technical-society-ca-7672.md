The Southern Pacific Historical and Technical Society preserves the history of the Southern Pacific Railroad (1865-1885). Their main focus is to aid scale model builders and industrial archaeologists. However, the society does operate a non-circulating archive, which is accessible to the public.

The society offers archive access.
