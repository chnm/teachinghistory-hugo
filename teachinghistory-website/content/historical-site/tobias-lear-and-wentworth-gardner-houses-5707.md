Built in 1760, the Wentworth-Gardner house is a fine example of the American Georgian style. The Lear House was built circa 1740 and is an example of the Georgian architectural style; the house is actually one of the most modest of Portsmouth's historic house museums and gives tourists a view of how the middle-to-upper-middle classes lived.

The houses offer tours.
