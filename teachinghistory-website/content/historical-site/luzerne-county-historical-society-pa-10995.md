The Luzerne County Historical Society is dedicated to preserving the history of Luzerne County, Pennsylvania. The society is headquartered in the Bishop Library, which also houses the society's collections. The society also owns and operates a local history museum, which has been in continuous operation since 1893, and the Swetland Home, which serves as a historic house museum. The Swetland Home has several period rooms, which span a period of over 70 years.
The society offers guided tours, exhibits, and special events. The website offers histories of the society, Bishop Library, Swetland Home, and local history museum. In addition, the website offers visitor information.
