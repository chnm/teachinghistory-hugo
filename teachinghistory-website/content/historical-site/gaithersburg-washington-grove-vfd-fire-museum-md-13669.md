The Gaithersburg - Washington Grove V.F.D. Fire Museum presents firefighting history from the 1920s to present day. Exhibits feature a 1941 pumper, a circa 1981 pumper, badges, helmets and uniforms, radios, department logs, and other relevant artifacts. Interactive consuls provide fire prevention information.
The museum offers exhibits.
