The Athens County Historical Society and Museum seeks to preserve and share the history of Athens County, Ohio. To this end, the society operates a museum of local history.

The society offers exhibits and interactive curriculum-based programs for students. Program participants receive pre-visit activity packets and access to a post-visit PowerPoint presentation.
