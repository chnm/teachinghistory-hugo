Located in Chicago's historic "Little Italy" neighborhood, the National Italian American Sports Hall of Fame preserves and promotes the history and heritage of Italian Americans in sports. Its collection aims to honor Italian Americans who have made lasting contributions to sports and society.

The hall offers exhibits.
