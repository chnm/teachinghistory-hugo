The Naugatuck Historical Society seeks to preserve and share the history of Naugatuck, Connecticut and the surrounding area. To this end, the society operates a museum of local history, located within the 1910 Naugatuck Railroad Station.
The society offers exhibits, outreach programs for students, and hands-on activities. Museum field trips are available by reservation only.
