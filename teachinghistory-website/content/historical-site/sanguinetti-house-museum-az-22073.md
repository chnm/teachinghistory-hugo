From the Arizona Historical Society [website](http://www.arizonahistoricalsociety.org/museums/):

"The Sanguinetti House Museum is in an 1870s adobe building and focuses on the history of the Lower Colorado River from the 1540s to the present."

The museum offers exhibits, guided tours, in-class outreach presentations, and educational materials for loan.
