The Sappington Cemetery State Historic Site houses the grave of a prominent area doctor, Dr. John Sappington, along with two Missouri governors, Gov. Meredith Marmaduke and Clairborne Jackson, who was driven from the state when the Civil War began.
The site offers guided tours. The website offers visitor information.
