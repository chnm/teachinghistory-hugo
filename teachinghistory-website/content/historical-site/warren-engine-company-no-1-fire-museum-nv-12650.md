The Warren Engine Company No. 1 Fire Museum presents the history of the Warren Engine Company No. 1. This company is an all-volunteer emergency medical and fire suppression support organization, founded in 1853, to bolster the Carson City Fire Department. Exhibits include historic firefighting equipment and photographs.

The museum offers exhibits.
