The Society's Museum, housed in the 1865 Christian Union Church, displays exhibits related to local history. The Society also operates the 1725 Southermost Schoolhouse and the 19th-century Old Town Hall.
The museum offers exhibits and research library access; the Old Town Hall offers exhibits.
