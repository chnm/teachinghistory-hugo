The Dewitt County Historical Museum presents the local history of Dewitt County, TX via an 1886 house museum. The museum contains period rooms, archives, historical costume, ranching artifacts, and domestic life artifacts. The museum grounds also hold a restored log cabin.

The museum offers exhibits and period rooms.
