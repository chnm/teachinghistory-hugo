The Mt. Horeb Area Historical Society seeks to preserve and share the history of Southwestern Dane County, Wisconsin. To this end, the society operates a museum. Collection highlights include textiles from the 1840s to present.
The museum offers exhibits and group tours. Reservations are required for group tours.
