The Redondo Beach Historical Society seeks to preserve and build awareness of the history of Redondo Beach, California. The society operates a museum containing information, artifacts, and memorabilia. This museum is located in a Queen Anne cottage built circa the 1890's.

The society offers a museum with exhibits.
