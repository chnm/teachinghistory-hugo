The Indiana Baseball Hall of Fame recognizes individuals who have contributed to the sport of baseball within the state of Indiana. Professional players, coaches, managers, contributors, and veterans are all eligible for the honor of induction. The hall maintains a museum.

The museum offers exhibits.
