The Oregon-California Trails Association seeks to preserve and heighten awareness of the history of emigrant trails and the people who made use of them.

The association website offers a virtual trail tour, historical information on a variety of topics pertinent to life on the trails, emigrant profiles, and information on locations and grave sites along the trail.
