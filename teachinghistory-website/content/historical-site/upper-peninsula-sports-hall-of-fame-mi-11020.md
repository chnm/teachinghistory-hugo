The Upper Peninsula Sports Hall of Fame is dedicated to memorializing the accomplishments of notable athletes from Michigan's Upper Peninsula.

The Hall of Fame offers tours. The website offers brief biographies of all inductees, along with visitor information and a brief history of the Hall of Fame.
