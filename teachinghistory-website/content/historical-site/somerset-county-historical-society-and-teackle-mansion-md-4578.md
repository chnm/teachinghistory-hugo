The Society operates Teackle Mansion, a Federal-era, Neoclassical style house built between 1802 and 1819 by Littleton Dennis Teackle in Princess Anne, Maryland.
The house offers tours and educational and recreational events.
\*\*Please note the mansion will be closed until June, 2011 due to restoration work\*\*\*
