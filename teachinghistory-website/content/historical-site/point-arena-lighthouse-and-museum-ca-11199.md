The 1908 Point Arena Lighthouse is one of the western coast's taller lighthouses at 115 feet in height. Prior to the implementation of electricity, maintaining the light's rotation required a 160-pound weight to be cranked to the highest point in the lighthouse every 75 minutes.
The lighthouse offers guided tours and self-guided grounds tours.
