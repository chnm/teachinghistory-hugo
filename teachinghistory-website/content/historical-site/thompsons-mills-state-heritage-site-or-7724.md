Thompson's Mills is the oldest water-powered grain mill in the state of Oregon, and chronicles the past 150 years of Oregon rural life.
The heritage site offers visitors a variety of interpretive displays, special events, and tours year round. The website offers a very brief history of the location, as well as visitor information.
