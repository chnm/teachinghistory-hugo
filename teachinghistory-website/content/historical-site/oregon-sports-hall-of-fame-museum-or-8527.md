The Oregon Sports Hall of Fame and Museum’s educational mission is to recognize and increase appreciation of Oregon’s athletic history. Their goal is for this legacy to inspire participation in sport and foster awareness of the values and life-long rewards gained from this participation.

The hall offers exhibits, films, scholarships, and interactive activities.
