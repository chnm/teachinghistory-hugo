* ### MN.5. Content Standard: Cognitive Development

  + #### 5.3. Performance Indicator:

    Social Systems Understanding
    - ##### 5.3.1. Grade Level Example: Human Relationships

      Recognize and appreciate similarities and differences between self and others from diverse backgrounds.
    - ##### 5.3.2. Grade Level Example: Human Relationships

      Understand various family roles, jobs, rules, and relationships.
    - ##### 5.3.3. Grade Level Example: Human Relationships

      Participate in activities to help others in the community.
    - ##### 5.3.4. Grade Level Example: Understanding the World

      Recognize and describe the roles of workers in the community.
    - ##### 5.3.5. Grade Level Example: Understanding the World

      Share responsibility in taking care of their environment.
    - ##### 5.3.6. Grade Level Example: Understanding the World

      Begin to recall recent and past events.
    - ##### 5.3.7. Grade Level Example: Understanding the World

      Identify characteristics of the places where they live and play within their community.
    - ##### 5.3.8. Grade Level Example: Understanding the World

      Begin to understand the uses of media and technology and how they affect their lives.
