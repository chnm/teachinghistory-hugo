This [video](http://web.archive.org/web/20220526104312/https://www.edutopia.org/kindergarten-project-based-learning-video), from the Auburn Early Education Center, AL, shows examples of using digital tools, such as [whiteboards](/digital-classroom/tech-for-teachers/21577), to encourage very young students to develop their own projects and to weave the use of electronics and the internet in with "real world" classroom activities.

Though the video does not describe specific techniques for whiteboard use, it demonstrates whiteboards working effectively for all age levels when incorporated into active learning environments.
