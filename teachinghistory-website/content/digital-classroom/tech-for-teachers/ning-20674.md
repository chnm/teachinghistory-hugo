## Overview

Ning is a social networking application and classroom management tool that enables users to create their own social network. The platform allows administrators to establish public or password-protected private networks with customizable membership controls. Core features include photo and video uploads, discussion forums, blogging capabilities, RSS feeds, calendar displays, and sub-group creation.

## Getting Started

The setup process is straightforward: sign up with your name, email address, and password. Create your network by naming it, adding a subtitle and description, and selecting privacy settings. You can customize layout and appearance before beginning to post content and inviting members to join. The platform supports various content types including photos, videos, blogs, events, and personal member pages.

## Example

Educators leverage Ning for professional collaboration and classroom administration. Most class-based networks remain password-protected for privacy. Teachers frequently post assignments, handouts, videos, photos, and maps while enabling student-created member pages for assignments and discussions.
