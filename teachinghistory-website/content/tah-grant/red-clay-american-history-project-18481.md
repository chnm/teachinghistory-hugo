Over three years, the project provides eight 2-day American history workshops and three 2-week summer institutes. The project focuses on teachers of grades 3-8 teaching American history as a single subject. Topics presented each year in the workshops and summer institutes are interrelated and complementary: year 1—topics on pre-Columbian America through the early national period of the United States to 1800; year 2—topics on Jeffersonian America through the Gilded Age; and year 3—topics on 20th century America. Partners are the American Philosophical Society, the Historical Society of Delaware, Delaware Public Archives, and the University of Delaware.
