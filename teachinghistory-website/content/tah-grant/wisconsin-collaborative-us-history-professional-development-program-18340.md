The district is partnering with the University of Wisconsin-LaCrosse, intermediate service unit CESA-12, and the National Council for History Education to train 20 middle and high school teachers. History faculty members from many universities will present a series of historical themes during the school year and in summer sessions. Curriculum specialists and professional development experts will lead a range of sessions and mentoring activities.
