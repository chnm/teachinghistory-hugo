Tunes, lyrics, recordings, sheet music—all are components of popular songs, and all can serve as evidence of peoples, places, and attitudes of the past. Written by Ronald J. Walters and John Spitzer, the [guide](http://historymatters.gmu.edu/mse/songs/) "Making Sense of American Popular Song" provides a place for students and teachers to begin working with songs as a way of understanding the past.
