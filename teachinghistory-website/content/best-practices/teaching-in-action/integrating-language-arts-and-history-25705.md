Karen Eanes combines language arts, art, and history in 4th-grade Virginia Studies. She hooks her students with engaging historical fiction before introducing them to nonfiction books and excerpts from primary sources. Finally, she asks them to apply their newfound knowledge to analyze Emanuel Gottlieb Leutze's *Washington Crossing the Delaware*. Using what they know, they question the reliability of the artist's depiction.
