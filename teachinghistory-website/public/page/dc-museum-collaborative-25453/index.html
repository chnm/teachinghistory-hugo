DC Museum Collaborative

The goal of this partnership is to bring together representatives from DC area museums and cultural organizations to talk about the wide array of professional development programs that are available to teachers, and talk about ways that we might better coordinate programs to provide more seamless field experiences for these groups when they are visiting the DC area.
