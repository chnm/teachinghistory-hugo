In 2005, Susan de la Paz published *Teaching Historical Reasoning and Writing: A Classroom Intervention*, one of the few experimental studies that investigated teaching for historical thinking in real classrooms. The study was part of a Teaching American History grant and involved 70 eighth graders, including 11 with learning disabilities. Their final essays were significantly better than those of 62 control students who did not have the curriculum.
