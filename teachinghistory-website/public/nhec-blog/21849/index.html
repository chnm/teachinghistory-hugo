Join the National History Education Clearinghouse (NHEC) on Facebook and Twitter! On Facebook, bookmark our page at http://tinyurl.com/historyclearinghouse. And on Twitter you can follow the latest postings online at http://twitter.com/teachinghistory or text to twitter@teachinghistory. New to Twitter? Check out our Twitter how-to posting in Tech for Teachers to learn more. Not yet on Facebook? Sign up for an account. We will post updates on new additions to the NHEC. We will also let you know about upcoming conferences where you can meet NHEC staff, learn more about the website, and let us know how we are doing.
