The 2009 Annual Teaching American History Project Directors Conference takes place in New York City, January 6–8, 2009, at the Hilton, New York, right on the heels of the Annual American History Association annual meeting. This year’s focus is Enhancing Project Quality, and the agenda includes presentations on historical content and pedagogical approaches in the classroom; project evaluation; and effective reporting and communication.
