  • ND.1. Content Standard: Skills and Resources

    Students apply Social Studies skills and resources.

    • K.1.1. Benchmark: Map Skills

      Identify land and water on a map or globe

  • ND.2. Content Standard: Important Historical Events

    Students understand important historical events.

    • K.2.1. Benchmark: Symbols

      Identify symbols of the United States (e.g., the flag, bald eagle, Statue of Liberty, patriotic songs, Pledge of Allegiance)

    • K.2.2. Benchmark: People and Events

      Identify United States holidays (e.g., Independence Day, Thanksgiving, Veterans’ Day, Memorial Day, Presidents’ Day, Martin Luther King, Jr. Day)

    • K.2.3. Benchmark: People and Events

      Identify Native Americans as the original inhabitants of North America

  • ND.3. Content Standard: Economic Concepts

    Students understand economic concepts and the characteristics of various economic systems.

    • K.3.1. Benchmark: Community Workers

      Identify community workers (e.g., librarian, doctor, teacher, police officer, fire fighter, custodian)

  • ND.4. Content Standard: Government and Citizenship

    Students understand the development, functions, and forms of various political institutions and the role of the citizen in government and society.

    • K.4.1. Benchmark: Citizenship

      Identify membership in different groups (e.g., family, school, community)

    • K.4.2. Benchmark: Citizenship

      Identify school rules and appropriate behavior

    • K.4.3. Benchmark: Citizenship

      Identify examples of good citizenship (e.g., honesty, cooperation, respect, responsibility)

  • ND.5. Content Standard: Concepts of Geography

    Students understand and apply concepts of geography.

    • K.5.1. Benchmark:

      No benchmark expectations at this level.

  • ND.6. Content Standard: Human Development and Behavior

    Students understand the importance of culture, individual identity, and group identity.

    • K.6.1. Benchmark: Identity

      State personal information (e.g., full name, age, telephone number)

    • K.6.2 Benchmark: Identity

      Identify characteristics that contribute to personal identity (e.g., physical characteristics, interests)

    • K.6.3 Benchmark: Identity

      Identify ways people are alike and different
